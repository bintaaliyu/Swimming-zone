    FAQ

    What level of fitness is required to participate in individual or group training ?

    We welcome participants of all fitness levels for both individual and group training. Whether you're a beginner or more experienced, our trainers will tailor the sessions to meet your needs.

    Do i need to have my own equipment ?

    You don't need to have your own equipment for most sessions. We provide the necessary gear, but if you have personal items you prefer to use, feel free to bring them along.

    How do i book a session ?

    You can book a session by visiting our website and using the online booking system, or you can call us directly at the front desk. We'll be happy to assist you with finding a time that works for you!

    Is it possible to organize children's sessions or lessons for children ?

    Yes, we offer organized sessions and lessons specifically for children! We have a variety of programs available, and you can inquire at the front desk or check our website for details.

    Do you organize tournaments and how do i participate in them?

    Yes, we organize tournaments, and to participate, you can check our schedule, register either at the reception or online, and pay any applicable fees.
